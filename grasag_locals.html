Local GRASAG Chapters

GRASAG currently operates local chapters in nineteen (19) public and private universities with representatives on all important boards/committees of their respective universities including the University Council. Each university has a local GRASAG Association that offer services to their own members.Executives of the local GRASAG Associations are elected by the graduate students of their respective universities. The following are the links to the various local GRASAG Chapters.

  • Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology
  • University of Ghana, Legon
  • University of Cape Coast
  • University for Development Studies
  • University of Education, Winneba
  • Takoradi Technical University
  • University of Mines and Technology
  • University of Energy and Natural Resources
