National Secretariat

The National Secretariat of GRASAG is located at the national capital and is made up of members of the National Executive Committee (NEC) elected annually by members at Congress. The NEC consists of the the President, the Vice-President, the Secretary, the Organising Coordinator, the Financial Controller, and the Gender Commissioner that maintain and manage the day-to-day operations of GRASAG National in accordance with the GRASAG constitution. The members of the NEC subject to the Constitution of GRASAG hold office for one term only.
