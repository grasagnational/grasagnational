Research

  • Doing Research
  • Research Funding
  • Research Repositories

Graduate students are involved in research across various disciplines cutting across subject areas such as health, technology, agriculture, engineering, physical sciences, humanities and business. All graduate students in Ghana are involved in creating and supporting the creation of new knowledge through research. Research is conducted in all graduate programmes of study. GRASAG National through the Research Committee has appropriate strategies and initiatives to enhance the research skills and experience of our members. Hence our motto, Partners in National development through research and practice. The following list of known repositories in member institutions has been compiled to promote the dissemination of knowledge among graduate students.
